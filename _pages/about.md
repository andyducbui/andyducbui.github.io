Andy Bui is a Senior Systems Engineer at Lockheed Martin Aeronautics supporting the F35 Joint Strike Fighter program. His work focuses on integrating navigation hardware and software and performing regression testing to ensure reliable performance across system updates and changes. 

After graduating from the University of Houston in 2020, Andy moved to the DFW area to begin his career at Lockheed Martin. He is currently attending SMU for a Masters in Data Science

Born in Southern California, Andy moved to Houston at age 15 and has called Texas home ever since. He is currently pursuing a master’s degree in Data Science at SMU to further his career, strengthen his foundation in AI, automation, and modern data driven engineering.
